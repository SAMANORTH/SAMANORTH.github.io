雅思5-2

Tue August 17, 2021 04:51 PM

今天完成了剑雅5-2的练习，发现自己的英语水平退步了，对句式结构不敏感，很多单词也忘记了，因此我决定尽量利用碎片时间背单词、看小站雅思的听力和阅读原文解析。小站雅思很好用。
关于听力：
这次section 2的题量比较大，不少于section3、4，所以我应该调整策略，拿到题目先浏览各个section的题量，优先做选择题≥5道的section。
up to…hours in advance类似于earlist time, overheads, meet overheads, strip down, consignment, slang, be intergrated with, indicator
关于阅读：
暂时忽略逗号之间的插入语
重要长句要把主干用括号圈出来
mould, derived, consitituent, viscous, impetus, pragmatic, substitues, dwindling, amassing dollars, extract, evenly, disdain, unparalleled, porous, relish, drab, fall from favour, seize on, in a similar way to, punchline, pointless, struck people as, pedigree, ludicrousness, settle on, incongruity, semantic, buzz, view from this angle, appeasement, cognitive, fairground, amused, circuit, tap into, prominence, emergent, magnetism, cartography, empirical, encyclopaedias, clung, exploitation, sealed, inadequacy, domain, perversely, foster, curator, narrative, transcript, inaugurated, genre, formative, momentum, privileged, overtaken by
先看标题，再根据题目浏览文章前几段，定位重要句，重点理解重要句及其上下文。
不论是听力还是阅读，做题专注最重要。
